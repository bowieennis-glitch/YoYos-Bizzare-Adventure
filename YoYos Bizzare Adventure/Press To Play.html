YoYo Shooting Plus

Manual

How to Play

Goal: survive waves and clear rounds.
Move: WASD
Aim: Mouse (or Arrow Aim in Options)
Attack: hold Left Mouse (some characters replace shooting with a melee/ability attack)
Abilities: Space, Q, E, R (varies by character)
Pause: Esc
Toggle controls (in-game): C
Upgrades: after each round you pick 1 upgrade.

Characters

Ryan
Main Attack: Slashes instead of normal shooting (hits in an arc).
Space: Dash (5s cooldown). Dashes in your move direction. Can hurt you if you dash through enemies.
Q: Dummy Bomb (15s cooldown). Drops a bomb at your position that explodes after ~3s (big AoE).
E: Sword Throw (5s cooldown). Throws a spinning sword that hits enemies on contact and then returns.
R: Nanami Mode. Starts with a 10s nerf (weaker/slower + take more damage), then a 20s buff (stronger/faster + take less damage), then returns to normal. During buff, each slash also stuns nearby enemies.
Jojo
Space: Throw YoYo (2s cooldown). Only 1 YoYo can be active at a time. It goes out, then returns.
Q: Walk the Dog (10s cooldown). Freezes the YoYo in place for up to ~5s, then it retracts.
E: Slingshot. When the YoYo is returning (or while it’s being held), queue a slingshot. The longer you hold during Walk the Dog, the stronger the launch when it returns to you.
R: Lock (20s cooldown). While Walk the Dog is active, lock yourself orbiting around the YoYo.
Chance
Base: Lower max HP than other characters.
Space: Coin Flip (2.5s cooldown). Heads gives Weakness stacks for 15s (each stack makes you take more damage and move slower). Tails recharges ability charges.
E: Throw Ace (uses charges, max 5). Throws a powerful card projectile with explosion.
Q: Drop Mine (uses charges, max 5). Drops a mine that seeks enemies and explodes on contact.
R: Stealth (uses charges, max 5). Become invincible for ~10s.
Slots: before each round you spin for temporary Damage / Fire Rate / HP bonuses.

Upgrades

When: after each round you pick 1 upgrade.
What they do: upgrades increase your stats like damage, fire rate, move speed, range, projectile speed, crit chance/damage, max HP, regen, life steal, pierce, rebound, explosion radius, and homing.
Luck: increases the chance of higher rarity upgrades.

Tips

Keep moving: enemy contact damage is dangerous if you get surrounded.
Try different control modes: Mouse Aim is precise; Arrow Aim can be easier on keyboard.
Upgrade synergies: Piercing + Explosion + Fire Rate can clear crowds fast.
Character learning: read the bottom-right HUD rings to see cooldowns/charges.
